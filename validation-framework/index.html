Interview Guide

Core Question Categories

Questions organized by validation area. Each category maps to specific assumptions we need to validate.

1

ICP & Persona Validation

Areas 1-2 | 8 questions
  • Walk me through your role in learning decisions at your organization. Who else is involved?
  • How does your company currently approach employee development? What triggers investment?
  • When you think about L&D vendors, what's the first thing that comes to mind?
  • What's the biggest frustration you have with your current learning setup?
What We Validate

Decision-making structure, buying committee composition, current pain awareness, vendor perception frame

2

Value Proposition Testing

Area 3 | 10 questions
  • If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about L&D in your organization, what would it be?
  • When L&D comes up in leadership meetings, what's the tone? Positive, skeptical, indifferent?
  • How do you currently measure whether learning investments are paying off?
  • "Learning only matters if it drives measurable business impact." Agree or disagree? Why?
  • What would make you confident that L&D is actually moving the needle on performance?
What We Validate

Pain severity ranking, POV resonance (especially POV 1: Impact), measurement maturity, executive perception of L&D value

3

Emotional Fractures Deep Dive

Area 4 | 12 questions
  • Tell me about a time you felt your L&D function wasn't getting the respect it deserves.
  • What keeps you up at night about your organization's ability to compete in 3-5 years?
  • How do you feel when you see competitors investing heavily in talent development?
  • Describe the gap between where you want L&D to be and where it is today.
  • When AI tools come up in conversations, what's your honest reaction?
What We Validate

5 emotional fractures: strategic invisibility, competitive anxiety, skills gap fear, transformation fatigue, AI disruption pressure

4

Buyer Journey Mapping

Areas 5-6 | 10 questions
  • When you first started researching L&D solutions, where did you look?
  • Do you ever use AI tools like ChatGPT to research business solutions? How?
  • What content or resources influenced your thinking before talking to vendors?
  • Walk me through a recent learning tech purchase from first awareness to decision.
  • Who in your organization would need to say yes before signing a contract?
What We Validate

Search behavior (Google vs AI), content influence by stage, buying committee roles, decision timeline, information sources trusted

5

Competitive Positioning

Area 7 | 8 questions
  • Which vendors did you consider or compare? What stood out about each?
  • What would make one LMS/LXP vendor clearly better than another in your eyes?
  • Have you heard of Studytube? What's your impression?
  • If I said "Studytube is the X of Y," what would X and Y be for you?
What We Validate

Competitive frame, differentiation clarity, category association, perceived positioning vs actual positioning

6

C-Level Resonance Testing

Area 8 | 8 questions
  • "Learning is the only sustainable competitive moat." How does that land with you?
  • What thought leadership content (podcasts, articles, events) do you consume around L&D?
  • If you could have a CEO roundtable on one L&D topic, what would it be?
  • What would make you share an article about learning with your board or leadership team?
What We Validate

POV resonance at executive level, content format preferences, topics that earn attention, sharing triggers

7

Proof Point Preferences

Area 9 | 4 questions
  • What kind of proof would convince you that an L&D investment was worth it?
  • When you read customer stories, what makes one credible and another feel like marketing?
  • What metrics would your CFO need to see before approving a significant L&D investment?
  • Who would you want to talk to (peer reference) before making a vendor decision?
What We Validate

Proof point hierarchy, credibility markers, metric expectations, peer influence weight
